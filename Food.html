Taste the Heart of Batanes

Explore the timeless flavors of Ivatan culture, dishes crafted from the land, the sea, and generations of tradition. Warm, simple, and deeply rooted in heritage.

Tatus (Coconut Crab)

Rich, naturally sweet, and traditionally served during special gatherings.

Vunes (Dried Taro Stalks)

Earthy and savory, cooked with pork and garlic — Ivatan comfort food.

Uvud (Banana Pith Meatballs)

Soft, delicate meatballs made from banana pith, pork, and fish.

Lunis (Cured Pork)

Salt-preserved pork slow-cooked to perfection — rich and unforgettable.

Payi

Sticky rice wrapped in fresh seaweed with salted fish or meat. A simple and flavorful taste of Batanes’ coastal tradition.
