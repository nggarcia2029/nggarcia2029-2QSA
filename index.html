Batanes

The Land of Rolling Hills & Calm Horizons

What Is Batanes Known For?

Batanes is loved for its peaceful landscapes, stone houses, vibrant Ivatan culture, dramatic cliffs, and postcard-worthy rolling hills. It is the calmest and safest province in the Philippines — a natural escape for anyone seeking serenity, adventure, and beauty.

Why Visit Batanes?

Pure serenity, breathtaking scenery, kind locals, and untouched nature everywhere you look.

Places to Explore

Basco Lighthouse, Marlboro Hills, Sabtang Island, Vayang Rolling Hills, and more iconic spots.

Hotels & Stays

Peaceful homestays, cozy inns, and cliffside lodges with ocean views and Ivatan hospitality.
