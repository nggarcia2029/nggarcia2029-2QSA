About the Authors

Meet the passionate team behind this Batanes travel project. Each of us brings a unique perspective and love for adventure, design, and storytelling.

Ysa

Curious and adventurous, Ysa explored Batanes through its food and famous destinations. She wrote about the must-visit tourist spots and local dishes, capturing the charm and culture of the islands in a warm and engaging way.

Isha
